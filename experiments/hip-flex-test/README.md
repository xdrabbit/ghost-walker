# Hip Flex Test

## Purpose

Validate whether cable-based assist improves leg lift and forward swing.

---

## Setup

- single leg
- waist anchor + thigh cuff
- manual trigger assist
- low tension start

---

## Procedure

1. Stand with support
2. Lift leg without assist
3. Lift leg with assist
4. Repeat multiple times
5. Adjust force and angle

---

## Record

- subjective effort (1-10)
- smoothness
- stability
- comfort
- any instability or surprise motion

---

## Goal

Answer one question:

> Does this make movement meaningfully easier?
